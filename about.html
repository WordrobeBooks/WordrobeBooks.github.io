About Wordrobe :

Wordrobe is a digital library for ebooks, audiobooks, e-magazines. It can be used to create a personal collection from various distributors and publishers.

Wordrobe's History

Wordrobe app is a culmination of the efforts of a team work of volunteers working diligently for more than 3 years. A brief timeline is given below.

2020 - Exploration

The journey started with identifying the limitations and challenges faced by Malayalam Islamic Publishers in bringing their books to a digital platform. Tools and processes were developed to make conversion and publishing of e-book as convenient as possible.

2021 - App Development

With the e-book creation flow sorted, the attention turned to publishing platforms. None of the established publishing platforms had the features or business model that would cater to the Malayalam Islamic Publishers. A decision was made to start development of an independent platform for publishing the e-books.

2023 - Initial Public Release of Apps

The Wordrobe app launched in Google Play Store and Apple App store.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to make reading and listening to Islamic literature accessible to everyone.
